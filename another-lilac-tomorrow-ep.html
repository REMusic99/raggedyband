Another Lilac Tomorrow
Another Lilac Tomorrow brings you three authentic, acoustic, and story-filled songs, starting with "Lilac", the sweet remembrances of a fantastic lilac bush. Featuring Bob on the mando and Lynn on guitar and vocals, this is an Americana favorite. Bob's tongue-in-cheek ode to the loss of a blanket on a chilly winter night (Another New Love Song) sets the toes tapping and lips curving up into a smile with its country-infused guitar playing. The EP ends with a sailing jaunt to a beach for a picnic among the palm trees and sparkling waters of an unnamed sea in "When Tomorrow Comes." Get Another Lilac Tomorrow Now!
