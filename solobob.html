Sing Along To Every Song
I'm Bob. I've been singing and playing guitar at senior communities for more than a dozen years, specializing in songs of the American Songbook from the 1920s through the 1950s and '60s, what many people call "oldies". These days, most crowds enjoy some Beatles, Elvis, and Hank Sr. sprinkled among the Frank Sinatra, Dean Martin, and Bing Crosby hits.

All music has a history and touches people in many different ways, so I engage listeners with facts and stories about the songs and singers. My repertoire also includes songs from the 60s through the 2010s, as well as original tunes recorded with my wife as the duo Raggedy Edge. Whether as a duo or solos (either one of us), we provide quality musical entertainment for discerning crowds or background music, with or without vocals, for dinners and other events.

For pricing and scheduling information, contact me through an email to bobfarleymusic@gmail.com or by contacting Lynn at 937-245-9165.
