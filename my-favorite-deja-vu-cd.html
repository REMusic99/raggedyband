Limited edition, signed by Lynn and Bob, sequentially numbered.

This CD featured keyboards and bass by W. John Houston, who also recorded, mixed and mastered the CD. Drummer/percussionist Dutch Workman provided the beat on several songs, too. The addition of these two master musicians gives My Favorite Déjà Vu an extra dose of rhythmicality listeners will enjoy.

Produced by Lynn Perdzock and Bob Farley
Additional production: Curtis Alley, Dave Gaylor, Lori Kryspin
Recorded March 2013 and July 2013 at Swami of the Three Studios, Hurricane, Utah, W. John Houston, engineer.
Lynn Perdzock--vocals, guitar, percussion
Bob Farley--guitar, vocals, bass
W. John Houston--bass, organ, piano
Darin "Dutch" Workman--drums, percussion
All songs written by Farley and Perdzock, except Playground Swings (Farley) and Driving Blind (Perdzock).
All songs copyright 2013 by Raggedy Edge, Lynn Perdzock, and Bob Farley
